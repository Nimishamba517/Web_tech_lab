Did Tilak Varma play Asia Cup 2025's best knock in final? Sunil Gavaskar reacts

'Tilak made every ball count for something'

Sunil Gavaskar said that the knock by Tilak Varma during the Asia Cup 2025 final against Pakistan was one of the best in the tournament. Tilak was the star of the show as he saved India from an early stutter and guided them to a five-wicket win to win the title. India were reduced to 20 for three at one point before the southpaw steadied the ship with Sanju Samson before stitching together a match-winner partnership with Shivam Dube.
